Deepwater Horizon

Several conspiracy theories surround the 2010 Gulf of Mexico oil-rig accident, suggesting that it was caused by sabotage from individuals promoting environmentalism, or a submarine strike by North Korea or Russia. These theories were partially promoted by US radio host Rush Limbaugh.

New Coke

According to a theory, The Coca-Cola Company deliberately replaced its superior formula with an inferior one in New Coke, either to create more demand for the original product or to reintroduce it later with a cheaper formula. However, Coca-Cola President Donald Keough denied the accusation, stating that the company was neither foolish nor clever enough to do so.
