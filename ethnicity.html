Antisemitism

Antisemitism has a long history of being associated with conspiracy theories, including the belief that Jews are responsible for controlling the world. Holocaust denial is also considered an antisemitic conspiracy theory. Reptilian conspiracies have become controversial for their antisemitic notions, including claims that prominent Jewish figures are reptilian shapeshifters.

Anti-Armenianism

Conspiracy theories in Azerbaijan claim that Armenians hold secret political power, with President Ilham Aliyev promoting these theories. Turkish Foreign Minister Mevlüt Çavuşoğlu believes that Russian media is run by Armenians, while American writer Samuel Weems and filmmaker Davud Imanov accuse Armenians of perpetrating hoaxes and plots against Azerbaijan.
